VisRx Privacy Policy

Welcome to VisRx! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to transparently explain how we collect, use, and protect your information, as well as the rights you have.

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all services provided by VisRx, including but not limited to our website, applications, and other platforms.

Collection and Use of Information

Collection of Personal Information

The information we collect includes email addresses, information provided by users upon account registration, and manually imported information such as age, gender, and blood pressure from Apple Health, as well as medication intake information.

Method of Collection

We collect personal information through information provided by users during account registration and subsequent manual imports by the users.

Purpose of Use

The collected information is mainly used to provide services such as medication reminders and guidance.

Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We may share anonymized user information and medication information with third-party large model providers, such as OpenAI, for computation purposes to provide the aforementioned services. We will not share your personal information with any other third parties unless required by law.

User Rights

Users can manually delete or change the imported information at any time. If you have any questions about managing your personal information, please contact us at visrx@outlook.com.

Information Security

We adopt industry-standard data encryption and communication algorithms to protect the security of your personal information and prevent information leakage, tampering, or loss.

Protection of Children and Minors

We recommend that our services are used by adults. The use by minors should be under the supervision of a guardian. We do not intentionally collect personal information from minors.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website and users will be notified before the changes take effect.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at visrx@outlook.com.

Thank you for choosing VisRx!
